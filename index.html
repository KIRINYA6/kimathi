About Brian Kirinya

With over 15 years of experience in case management, Brian has helped hundreds of clients navigate complex systems and achieve their goals.

  • Senior Case Manager at Hope Services (2015-Present)
  • Case Management Supervisor at Community Care (2010-2015)
  • Case Worker at Family Support Services (2005-2010)
  • Specialized training in trauma-informed care and crisis intervention

Brian's educational background provides a strong foundation for his case management practice.

  • Master of Social Work (MSW), University of Washington
  • Bachelor of Arts in Psychology, Seattle University
  • Certified Case Manager (CCM) credential
  • Ongoing professional development in mental health first aid and cultural competency

Brian's approach to case management is client-centered and strengths-based.

  • Focuses on empowering clients to achieve self-sufficiency
  • Collaborative approach with other service providers
  • Committed to cultural humility and accessibility
  • Evidence-based practices tailored to individual needs
  • Strong emphasis on building trust and rapport
